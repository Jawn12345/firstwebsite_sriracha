Sriracha is a go-to sauce for a lot of dishes. It is great on burgers, noodles, rice and many other dishes. Why have a Ketchup versus Mayo debate when Sriracha is clearly the winner. Everyone should have some spice in their lives. Sriracha is super HOT, so hot that other brands have incorporated Sriracha into their own sauces. Heinz, a famous condiment known for their Ketchup, created a Sriracha flavored Ketchup. Spicy Mayo? Literally Mayonnaise mixed with Sriracha, and the only acceptable form of Mayo. There are a lot of benefits with adding Sriracha to almost everything that we eat.

Happiness

When the human tongue comes into contact with spices and spicy sauces, like Sriracha, the receptors in both the tongue and the nose activates a sensation of pain and heat. The body then releases endorphins, dopamine, and serotonin.
Endorphins mainly focuses on dealing with stress and reducing pain, like after the body comes into contact with something spicy. Endorphins are essentially natural painkillers and can leave you with a "high" as response to combat the pain. These hormones are also release during other pleasurable activities, such as exercise and sex.
Serotonin, on the other hand, deals with stabilizing mood. It helps with reducing stress and anger, and it even helps with depression.
Lastly, dopamine is released when we are doing something pleasurable. All of these hormones serve to create happiness within ourselves and our body.

Health Benefits

Eating spicy food can be beneficial for your body. Studies have shown that eating spice can help boost heart health, lose weight, and aid in digestion. Spices have been found to be able to make people fill full faster, reducing the amount of food intake and prevent overeating. The chillis in Sriracha contain capsaicin, which helps reduce inflammation and boost metabolism.

Practicality

Sriracha is one of the milder hot sauces out there. You can enjoy some happiness without experiencing too much of the pain. It also contains natural preservatives that allow the sauce to stay fresh for a long period of time.

Conclusion

As a wise man once said, "no pain no gain" and "no pain no pleasure." Now go out and buy a bottle of Sriracha, if you don't own one already to spice up your life, to improve your happiness and health.
